BRAND GUIDELINES · v1.0
the ai[hack]club
brand system
Everything you need to represent AI HackClub — a community of builders shipping with AI. Logos, colors, type, and the rules that keep it consistent.
01
logo
The logotype is set in a chunky lowercase grotesque with square brackets around hack and a trailing orange underscore. Never redraw, restretch, recolor the letters, or alter the bracket spacing.

04
motifs
join us
the underscore
The orange cursor. Fixed 2.6 : 1 ratio, always #eb5b0c, sits on the baseline. Ends brand statements — once per composition.
open [build] hours
the brackets
Square brackets highlight one key word — [hack], [build], [demo]. Typographic or as drawn corner frames. Max once per layout.
05
usage
DO_
Keep headings lowercase, verbs first.
Use orange as a single accent.
Let layouts breathe — one focal point.
Separate with borders, not shadows.
DON'T_
Title Case Every Heading.
Recolor or stretch the logotype.
Add gradients, glows, or emoji.
Fill a whole layout with orange.
